Design and Aesthetics


Jewelry should have a visually appealing design that matches the wearer's style and preferences. This includes the overall look, symmetry, and the artistry involved in its creation.

Material Quality


High-quality materials, such as precious metals (gold, silver, platinum) and genuine gemstones, ensure durability and long-lasting beauty. The purity of metals and the authenticity of stones are critical.

Craftsmanship


Excellent craftsmanship is essential. This involves precise detailing, secure settings for gemstones, and a smooth finish. Handcrafted jewelry often stands out due to the attention to detail.
